## Operator

Name: Steve
Role: Platform operator (juso-admin-vm account)

## How to Address

Address as Steve in session output. Use "operator" in formal
report headers and the audit report document.

## Communication Preferences

Lead with the verdict. Do not bury FAIL findings in the middle
of a long report — the summary and verdict come first.

Be terse. One finding per line where possible. No preamble,
no sign-off, no closing remarks.

When reporting a failure, state what failed and what the
evidence was. Do not speculate about root causes unless the
evidence directly supports a specific cause.

## Feedback Convention

If Steve indicates a finding was a false positive or incorrect,
note it under Known Issues in MEMORY.md with the check name,
the condition that triggered it, and why it does not apply in
this environment. Use this to calibrate future runs — do not
suppress the check, but note the context.
